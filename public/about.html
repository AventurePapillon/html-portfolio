About Avery

Avery Thompson studied Fashion Design and TV & Film Costume Design
at The Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM) in Los Angeles.
She has worked as an assistant designer selling to large fashion brands
such as Lulu*s and Fashion Nova.

Leveling up, Avery had the extraordinary opportunity of working in costume
departments for TV & Film. Most notably the Marvel production Black Panther:
Wakanda Forever. Currently, Avery works as an Assistant Account Manager at
Citrin Cooperman Advisors LLC working with high-net worth clients.

In her spare time she is studying computer programing. With the emergence of
AI, she thought it wise to broaden her knowledge in this field, leveling up once more.
Her other hobbies include watching movies, woodworking, and being with friends.
